pandu sastrowardoyo

aka Decentricity

biography

pandu sastrowardoyo, known as decentricity, is a prominent figure in the blockchain and tech world. she was born on june 2, 1984, and has an educational background in environmental engineering from institut teknologi bandung. her programming journey began at age six, fostering a deep interest in technology.

she has worked at ibm and procter & gamble before dedicating herself to the blockchain industry. she co-founded blockchain zoo and blocksphere, is actively involved with the indonesian blockchain association, and founded debio network, a decentralized platform for medical and bioinformatics data ensuring user privacy and data security using blockchain.

she also created myriad.social, aiming to revolutionize social media through decentralization, emphasizing user control and freedom of speech. additionally, she is recognized for her work with debio network, leveraging ipfs for identity and data storage and promoting blockchain's potential to transform industries and societal functions.
